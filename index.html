ABOUT

SSGYP (Sarv Sanyukt Gramin Yojana Portal) is a portal where all the schemes of the government can be easily accessed by the people. There are several schemes provided by the government for the wellfare of under privileged civillians of our country. The main objective of this website is to put all the different schemes all together at a single place so people don't get much trouble in finding the perticular scheme they are looking for. Schemes are sorted into four major categories on this website. These are Educational Schemes, Agricultural Schemes, Financial Schemes and Medical Schemes. Other than this we are currently working on developing more accurate filter so people can find their schemes more easily.
